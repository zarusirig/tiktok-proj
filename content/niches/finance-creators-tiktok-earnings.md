# Finance Creators TikTok Earnings Guide (2025 Data)

## Introduction

Finance is TikTok's highest-earning content niche by RPM and brand deal rates. Finance creators (FinTok) earn premium rates due to high-value audiences and lucrative financial services partnerships.

---

## Why Finance Creators Earn More

**Premium audience:**
- Higher disposable income
- Active purchase intent (investing, banking, credit cards)
- Advertisers pay 2–3× more for finance viewers

**Creator Fund RPM:**
- Finance: $0.03–$0.05 per 1K views
- General content: $0.01–$0.02 per 1K views

## Earnings by Follower Tier

### 10K–50K Followers
**Monthly earnings:** $1,000–$5,000
- Brand deals: $500–$2,000/post
- Affiliate (credit cards, investing apps): $500–$2,000

### 50K–100K Followers
**Monthly earnings:** $5,000–$20,000
- Brand deals: $2,000–$8,000/post
- Affiliate: $2,000–$10,000 (high-value conversions)

### 100K+ Followers
**Monthly earnings:** $20,000–$150,000+
- Brand deals: $8,000–$50,000/post
- Affiliate: $10,000–$50,000+
- Courses/Memberships: $5,000–$50,000

## Top Earning Opportunities

### 1. Credit Card Affiliate Programs
**Highest-paying affiliate niche:**
- $50–$500 per approved application
- Single video can earn $5,000–$20,000
- Top programs: Chase, American Express, Capital One

### 2. Investing App Partnerships
- Robinhood, Webull, M1 Finance
- $10–$50 per sign-up
- Sponsored campaigns: $3,000–$30,000

### 3. Financial Services Brand Deals
- Banks, fintech companies, insurance
- $5,000–$50,000 per campaign
- Often multi-video series

### 4. Online Courses/Coaching
- "Learn to invest" courses: $100–$500
- 1-on-1 financial coaching: $200–$1,000/hour
- Membership sites: $20–$100/month per member

## Monetization Strategies

### Strategy 1: Focus on Evergreen Content
- "How to build credit"
- "Investing for beginners"
- Content earns for years (affiliate commissions continue)

### Strategy 2: Build Email List
- Offer free financial guide
- Promote courses and affiliate offers to list
- $10,000–$50,000 additional annual income

### Strategy 3: Create Comparison Content
- "Best credit cards for [category]"
- High conversion (viewers ready to apply)
- Each affiliate link can earn $100–$500/conversion
