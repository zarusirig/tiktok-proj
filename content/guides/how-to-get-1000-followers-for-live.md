## Understanding TikTok's Algorithm

Before jumping into tactics, understand what TikTok rewards:

###

 The For You Page (FYP) Algorithm

**TikTok prioritizes:**
1. **Watch time** (how long viewers watch your video)
2. **Completion rate** (% who watch to the end)
3. **Engagement** (likes, comments, shares, saves)
4. **Re-watches** (viewers watching multiple times)
5. **Profile visits** (video drives viewers to check your profile)

**TikTok doesn't heavily weigh:**
- Follower count (even 0-follower accounts can go viral)
- Past video performance (each video gets fresh chance)
- Posting frequency alone (quality > quantity)

**Key insight:** Every video gets shown to a small "test audience" (200‚Äì500 viewers). If it performs well with them, TikTok shows it to more people. This means even new accounts can gain followers fast with the right content.
