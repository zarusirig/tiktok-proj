## FAQ

### Can I use VPN to access Creator Fund from ineligible country?
**No.** TikTok detects actual location through multiple methods. VPN use violates Terms of Service and results in account suspension. Don't risk your account.

### If I move to an eligible country, can I join Creator Fund?
**Yes,** if you:
- Legally reside in eligible country
- Have local payment method (bank account or PayPal)
- Provide valid tax documentation
- Meet all other requirements (10K followers, 100K views)

### Can I apply if I have dual citizenship?
**Yes.** Apply using citizenship of eligible country. Provide appropriate tax documentation and payment method from that country.

### Does TikTok announce new country eligibility in advance?
Usually no. Most expansions are announced 1â€“2 weeks before launch, or simultaneously with launch.

### How can I stay updated on new country eligibility?
- Follow TikTok Newsroom
- Join TikTok creator communities (Reddit, Facebook groups)
- Enable notifications in TikTok Creator Portal
- Check this page regularly (we update within 24 hours of TikTok announcements)
