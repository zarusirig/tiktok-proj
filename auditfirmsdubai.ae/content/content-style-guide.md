# Content Style Guide - Elite Audit Dubai

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Characteristics
- **Professional**: Authoritative yet approachable
- **Trustworthy**: Accurate, fact-based, transparent
- **Expert**: Demonstrating deep knowledge of Dubai audit landscape
- **Clear**: Complex topics explained simply
- **Action-Oriented**: Focus on solutions and outcomes

### Tone Variations by Content Type

#### Service Pages (Commercial)
- Confident and direct
- Benefits-focused
- Urgency without pressure
- Professional reassurance

**Example**:
"Get your statutory audit completed in 7 days. Our Ministry-approved auditors ensure full compliance with UAE regulations while minimizing disruption to your business."

#### Educational Content (Informational)
- Helpful and informative
- Patient and thorough
- Neutral and objective
- Encouraging learning

**Example**:
"Understanding IFRS requirements in the UAE can seem complex, but breaking it down into key components makes compliance manageable. Let's explore what your business needs to know."

#### Regulatory Updates (News)
- Timely and accurate
- Clear implications
- Action-oriented advice
- Authoritative interpretation

**Example**:
"The latest Federal Decree-Law No. 41 of 2023 introduces significant changes to audit requirements. Here's what this means for your business and the steps you need to take."

## Writing Guidelines

### Sentence Structure
- **Average length**: 15-20 words
- **Paragraph length**: 2-3 sentences
- **Flesch Reading Ease**: 50-60 (accessible to business audience)
- **Active voice**: 80% minimum
- **Transition words**: 30% of sentences

### Formatting Standards

#### Headings
```
# H1: One per page, includes primary keyword
## H2: Major sections, include LSI keywords
### H3: Subsections, natural language
#### H4: Lists or minor points (rarely used)
```

#### Lists
- Use bullet points for unordered information
- Use numbered lists for sequential steps
- Keep list items parallel in structure
- Limit to 7 items maximum

### SEO Content Requirements

#### Keyword Integration
- **Primary keyword**: 
  - Title tag
  - H1
  - First 100 words
  - 3-5 times naturally in body
  - Meta description
  
- **Secondary keywords**: 
  - H2 headings
  - Throughout body (2-3 times each)
  - Image alt text
  - Internal anchor text

- **LSI keywords**: 
  - Natural integration
  - 20-30 unique variations
  - Context-appropriate usage

#### Entity Mentions
Required entities per content type:
- **Service pages**: 15-20 entity mentions
- **Pillar content**: 30+ entity mentions
- **Blog posts**: 10-15 entity mentions
- **Location pages**: 20+ local entity mentions

Core Entities to Include:
- Dubai / UAE (location)
- Ministry of Economy (regulator)
- IFRS / ISA (standards)
- VAT / Corporate Tax (compliance)
- Free Zones (DIFC, DMCC, etc.)
- Audit types (statutory, internal, etc.)

### Dubai Audit Terminology

#### Industry-Specific Terms
- **Statutory Audit**: Always capitalize, link to service page
- **External Auditor**: Use instead of "outside auditor"
- **Financial Statements**: Not "financial reports"
- **Audit Report**: Not "audit certificate"
- **Management Letter**: Specific term for recommendations
- **Engagement Letter**: Contract for services
- **Materiality**: Technical term, explain on first use
- **Substantive Testing**: Audit procedure terminology

#### UAE-Specific Terminology
- **Mainland Company**: Companies under DED
- **Free Zone Entity**: Not "free zone company"
- **Trade License**: Not "business license"
- **Ejari**: Tenancy contract registration
- **MOA/AOA**: Memorandum/Articles of Association
- **MOHRE**: Ministry of Human Resources
- **FTA**: Federal Tax Authority
- **Economic Substance Regulations**: ESR compliance

#### Regulatory References
Format: "Federal Law No. XX of YEAR"
- Federal Law No. 32 of 2021 (Commercial Companies Law)
- Federal Decree-Law No. 47 of 2022 (Corporate Tax)
- Federal Decree-Law No. 41 of 2023 (Audit Profession)
- Cabinet Resolution No. 31 of 2021 (Beneficial Ownership)

### Meta Description Formula

#### Service Pages
"[Service] in Dubai by Ministry-approved auditors. [Benefit]. [Timeframe/USP]. [CTA]. ✓ IFRS experts ✓ [Location served]"

Example: "External audit services in Dubai by Ministry-approved auditors. Ensure compliance with UAE regulations. 7-day turnaround. Get free quote. ✓ IFRS experts ✓ All Emirates"

#### Location Pages
"[Location] audit firm serving [area specifics]. [Services offered]. [Unique value]. [CTA]. ✓ Licensed auditors ✓ Local expertise"

#### Guide Pages
"Complete guide to [topic] in Dubai/UAE. Learn [key benefit]. [Year] updated. [Reading time]. Expert insights on [specific aspect]."

### Title Tag Formulas

#### Service Pages
Primary Pattern: "[Service] in Dubai | [Benefit] | Elite Audit"
Alternative: "[Service] Dubai - [USP] | Elite Audit UAE"

#### Location Pages
Primary: "Audit Firm in [Location] | Elite Audit Dubai"
Alternative: "[Location] Auditors | Audit Services | Elite Audit"

#### Guide Pages
Primary: "[Topic] in UAE: Complete Guide [Year]"
Alternative: "How to [Action] in Dubai | Step-by-Step Guide"

## Content Templates

### Opening Paragraph Template (Service Pages)
```
Looking for [service] in Dubai? [Company] provides [specific service description] 
to help [target audience] achieve [primary benefit]. With [credibility marker] and 
[unique value proposition], we ensure [key outcome] while [addressing pain point].
```

### Trust Signal Integration
Include 3-5 per page:
- "Ministry of Economy approved"
- "DFSA/ADGM registered"
- "[X] years serving Dubai"
- "[X]+ successful audits completed"
- "IFRS certified professionals"
- "Same-day response guarantee"

### Call-to-Action Variations

#### Soft CTAs (Educational Content)
- "Learn more about our audit services"
- "Discover how we can help"
- "Explore our solutions"

#### Direct CTAs (Service Pages)
- "Get your free audit quote"
- "Start your audit today"
- "Book a consultation now"

#### Urgent CTAs (Deadline-Driven)
- "Deadline approaching? We can help"
- "Fast-track your audit - 7 days"
- "Emergency audit services available"

## Style Consistency Rules

### Capitalization
- Company names: Always capitalize
- Service names: Lowercase unless proper noun
- Regulatory bodies: Always capitalize
- Free zones: Capitalize (DIFC, DMCC)

### Numbers
- Spell out one through nine
- Use numerals for 10 and above
- Always use numerals for:
  - Percentages (5%)
  - Money (AED 50,000)
  - Days/timeframes (7-day audit)
  - Statistics (3x faster)

### Dates and Times
- Date format: DD Month YYYY (14 November 2024)
- Time format: 24-hour (09:00-18:00)
- Deadline references: "before 31 March 2025"

### Currency
- Primary: AED (United Arab Emirates Dirham)
- Format: AED 50,000 (not 50,000 AED)
- No cents unless necessary
- Use "million" for large numbers (AED 10 million)

## Prohibited Practices

### Never Use
- ❌ Guaranteed rankings promises
- ❌ Unsubstantiated superlatives ("best", "top")
- ❌ Competitor bashing
- ❌ Legal advice (we're not lawyers)
- ❌ Specific tax advice
- ❌ Outdated regulatory information
- ❌ Keyword stuffing
- ❌ Duplicate content
- ❌ Plagiarized text
- ❌ False credentials

### Always Avoid
- Passive voice excess
- Jargon without explanation
- Walls of text
- Generic filler content
- Spelling/grammar errors
- Broken links
- Outdated information
- Unverified claims
