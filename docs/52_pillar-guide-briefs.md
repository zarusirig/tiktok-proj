# Pillar Guide Briefs

## Purpose

Provides detailed content briefs for long-form pillar content pages that establish topical authority and serve as hub pages for semantic SEO clusters.

## Owner

**Primary:** Content Lead
**Collaborators:** SEO Strategist, Senior Writer

## Strategic Disciplines

- **Systems Analysis** — Hub-spoke content architecture
- **Pattern Recognition** — SERP patterns, user intent analysis
- **Structured Problem-Solving** — Content brief specifications

---

## Brief Template

Each pillar guide brief includes:
1. **Target Keyword & Intent**
2. **Target Audience & Persona**
3. **Word Count Target**
4. **Required Sections**
5. **Internal Linking Strategy**
6. **SEO Requirements**
7. **Content Angle/Differentiation**
8. **Research Sources**
9. **Success Metrics**

## Pillar Guide 1: How to Make Money on TikTok

### Target Keyword
**Primary:** "how to make money on tiktok"
**Volume:** 60K/month | **Difficulty:** High | **Intent:** Informational

**Secondary Keywords:**
- TikTok monetization
- Ways to earn on TikTok
- TikTok income streams
- TikTok creator earnings

### Target Audience
**Primary Persona:** Aspiring Creator (18-24, 1K-50K followers)
**Secondary:** Scaling Creator researching monetization options

### Word Count
**Target:** 10,000 words
**Sections:** 15-20 major sections

### Required Sections

1. **Introduction** (300 words)
   - Hook: Real creator earnings example
   - Overview of monetization landscape 2025
   - What this guide covers

2. **TikTok Creator Fund** (1,200 words)
   - How it works
   - Eligibility requirements
   - How much you can earn
   - Pros and cons
   - [Link to Creator Fund Calculator]

3. **Brand Deals & Sponsorships** (1,500 words)
   - How brands find creators
   - TikTok Creator Marketplace overview
   - Rate ranges by follower tier
   - How to pitch brands
   - Contract considerations
   - [Link to Brand Deal Calculator]

4. **TikTok LIVE** (1,000 words)
   - LIVE Gifts explained
   - LIVE Subscriptions (badges)
   - How much creators earn
   - Strategies to maximize LIVE income
   - [Link to LIVE Gifts Calculator]

5. **TikTok Shop & Affiliate** (1,200 words)
   - TikTok Shop for creators
   - Affiliate commission structure
   - How to join
   - Best products to promote
   - [Link to Shop Commission Calculator]

6. **Additional Monetization** (800 words)
   - TikTok Series (paywalled content)
   - TikTok Pulse (ad revenue sharing)
   - Selling digital products
   - Driving traffic to external platforms

7. **Requirements & Eligibility** (600 words)
   - Summary table of all requirements
   - Timeline from 0 to monetization

8. **How Much Can You Really Earn?** (1,000 words)
   - Realistic earnings by follower tier
   - Case studies (3-5 creators)
   - Earnings breakdown examples

9. **Maximizing Your Earnings** (800 words)
   - Diversification strategy
   - Optimization tips per stream
   - Common mistakes to avoid

10. **Tax Considerations** (500 words)
    - 1099 income reporting
    - Deductible expenses
    - Working with accountant

11. **FAQ** (800 words)
    - 10-15 common questions

12. **Getting Started Checklist** (300 words)
    - Action items for new creators

### Internal Linking Strategy
- 8+ calculators linked contextually
- 5+ related guides linked
- 3+ comparison pages linked
- Breadcrumb: Home > Guides > How to Make Money on TikTok

### SEO Requirements
- Target keyword in H1, URL, first 100 words
- Schema: Article, FAQPage, HowTo
- Meta description: 155 characters, includes "2025" for freshness
- OG image: Custom graphic with earnings stats
- 10+ H2 headings with semantic variations

### Content Angle
**Differentiation:**
- Comprehensive (covers ALL monetization streams, not just Creator Fund)
- Data-driven (specific numbers, not vague "you can earn money")
- Updated for 2025 (TikTok Shop, Series, Pulse all included)
- Interactive (calculators embedded for each stream)

### Research Sources
- TikTok Creator Portal official docs
- Creator surveys (internal)
- Competitor content analysis (calculatecreator.com, etc.)
- Industry reports (Influencer Marketing Hub, Social Media Today)
- Reddit: r/TikTokCreators

### Success Metrics
- Rank top 3 for primary keyword within 6 months
- 5,000+ monthly organic visitors
- 3+ minutes avg time on page
- 50+ internal link clicks
- 5+ backlinks earned organically

## Content Production Workflow

1. **SEO Research** → Keyword analysis, SERP analysis, competitor gap analysis
2. **Brief Creation** → Content Lead creates detailed brief using template
3. **Outline Approval** → Product + SEO review outline
4. **First Draft** → Senior Writer produces draft
5. **Subject Matter Expert Review** → Product Manager fact-checks
6. **SEO Review** → SEO Strategist optimizes
7. **Editorial Review** → Content Lead final edit
8. **Development** → Handoff to dev team
9. **QA** → Content + Dev QA
10. **Launch** → Publish + promote
