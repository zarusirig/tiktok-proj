## Brand Voice

### Voice Attributes

**Professional yet approachable**
- Expert without being condescending
- Data-driven without being cold
- Educational without being boring

**Empowering**
- "You can do this" mentality
- Practical, actionable advice
- Celebrates creator success

**Transparent**
- Shows methodology openly
- Admits limitations
- Cites sources

**Objective**
- Platform-neutral (not TikTok cheerleading)
- Honest about challenges
- Balanced pros/cons

## Writing Principles

### 1. Clarity First

**Use simple language:**
- Write at 8th-9th grade reading level
- Define jargon on first use
- Short sentences (average 15â€“20 words)
- Active voice preferred

**Good:**
> "Your engagement rate measures how actively your audience interacts with your content. Higher engagement typically leads to better algorithmic performance and higher earnings."

**Bad:**
> "The engagement rate metric serves as a quantification methodology for determining the proportional interactive participation coefficient of one's audience constituency."

### 2. Be Specific

**Use concrete examples:**
- Real numbers
- Specific scenarios
- Named examples

**Good:**
> "A creator with 50,000 followers and 5% engagement earning $300/month from the Creator Fund could earn $1,500â€“$3,000 for a single brand deal."

**Bad:**
> "Creators can earn more from brand deals than the Creator Fund."

### 3. Show, Don't Just Tell

**Use tables, charts, examples:**

**Good:**
| Follower Count | Avg Monthly Earnings |
|----------------|----------------------|
| 10Kâ€“50K        | $50â€“$150             |
| 50Kâ€“100K       | $150â€“$450            |
| 100Kâ€“500K      | $500â€“$2,000          |

**Bad:**
> "Earnings increase as you grow."

### 4. Admit What You Don't Know

**Be honest about limitations:**

**Good:**
> "These estimates are based on reported averages from 500+ creators surveyed in Q4 2024. Your actual earnings may vary based on factors we can't fully predict, including TikTok's algorithm changes and audience location."

**Bad:**
> "This calculator is 100% accurate and will tell you exactly what you'll earn."

## Grammar & Style Rules

### Capitalization

**Product names:**
- TikTok (not Tiktok or tiktok)
- Creator Fund (capitalized)
- LIVE (all caps when referring to TikTok LIVE)
- For You Page (capitalized, can abbreviate as FYP after first use)

**Headings:**
- Sentence case: "How to calculate engagement rate"
- Not title case: "How To Calculate Engagement Rate"

### Numbers

**Spell out:**
- One through nine: "five calculators"
- Round numbers: "a thousand followers"

**Use numerals:**
- 10 and above: "15 monetization strategies"
- Percentages: "5% engagement rate"
- Currency: "$150"
- Statistics: "3Ã— more engagement"

### Formatting

**Bold:** Key terms on first use, important takeaways
**Italic:** Emphasis (use sparingly)
**Code blocks:** Formulas, technical terms
**Quotes:** Direct citations only

## Content Structure Standards

### Every Page Includes

1. **Clear H1:** What is this page?
2. **Intro paragraph:** What will you learn? (100â€“150 words)
3. **Skimmable structure:** Headings, bullets, short paragraphs
4. **Actionable takeaways:** What should reader do next?
5. **Internal links:** 3â€“5 relevant pages
6. **Last updated date:** Build trust

### Calculator Pages Include

- Input field labels (clear, specific)
- Helper text (explain what to enter)
- Tooltips (for complex terms)
- Results interpretation (what do numbers mean?)
- Methodology section (how we calculate)
- FAQ section (answer common questions)

### Guide Pages Include

- Table of contents (2,000+ words)
- Introduction (hook + value prop)
- Body sections (clear headings, examples)
- Key takeaways box
- FAQ section
- Related content links

### Target Keyword Integration

**Natural integration:**
- In H1 (page title)
- In first 100 words
- In 1â€“2 H2 headings
- 3â€“5 times in body (natural, not forced)
- In meta description

- TikTok monetization
- Creator earnings
- TikTok income
- RPM (Revenue Per Mille)

**Anchor text:** Descriptive, keyword-relevant

**Good:**

**Bad:**
