## Search Intent Mapping

### Informational Intent
**User Goal:** Learn about topic
**Content Type:** Comprehensive guides, explainers
**Examples:**
- How TikTok Creator Fund works
- What is RPM on TikTok
- TikTok monetization explained

**Content Requirements:**
- 4,000–10,000 words
- Clear definitions
- Examples and case studies
- Visuals/diagrams
- Table of contents
- FAQ section

### Transactional Intent
**User Goal:** Use a tool/calculator
**Content Type:** Interactive calculators
**Examples:**
- TikTok earnings calculator
- Engagement rate calculator
- Brand deal rate calculator

**Content Requirements:**
- Instant results (<1s)
- Clear input labels
- Results with context
- Methodology explanation below
- Related calculators suggested
- CTA to related content

### Commercial Intent
**User Goal:** Research before decision
**Content Type:** Comparison guides, benchmark data
**Examples:**
- TikTok vs YouTube earnings
- Brand deal rate benchmarks
- Best niches for monetization

**Content Requirements:**
- Data tables and charts
- Pros/cons lists
- Scenario comparisons
- Authoritative citations
- Clear recommendations
- Neutral tone

### Navigational Intent
**User Goal:** Find specific page/tool
**Content Type:** Landing pages, tool pages
**Examples:**
- TikTok Creator Fund calculator
- TikTok monetization guide
- [Brand] TikTok earnings

**Content Requirements:**
- Clear page title matching query
- Fast load time
- Prominent CTA/tool
