# Personas and Jobs-to-be-Done

## Purpose

Defines target user personas, their motivations, pain points, and the jobs they're hiring our platform to accomplish. Ensures all product, design, and content decisions are user-centered.

## Owner

**Primary:** Product Manager
**Collaborators:** UX Researcher, Designer, SEO Strategist, Content Lead

## Strategic Disciplines

- **Pattern Recognition** — User behavior patterns, decision-making triggers, search intent
- **Systems Analysis** — User journey mapping, touchpoint optimization
- **Structured Problem-Solving** — Jobs-to-be-done framework application

---

## Jobs-to-be-Done Framework

When creators visit our platform, they're "hiring" it to accomplish specific jobs:

**Functional Jobs:** Practical tasks (calculate earnings, estimate growth)
**Emotional Jobs:** How they want to feel (confident, informed, validated)
**Social Jobs:** How they want to be perceived (professional, data-driven)

---

## Primary Personas

### Persona 1: The Aspiring Creator

**Demographics:**
- Age: 18–24
- Followers: 1,000–50,000
- Content: Lifestyle, comedy, dancing, POV
- Location: United States, UK, Canada, Philippines, India
- Device: 90% mobile

**Psychographics:**
- Growth-focused, experimenting with content
- Curious about monetization potential
- Limited understanding of TikTok's monetization models
- Motivated by peer success stories
- Income goal: Side income ($100–$500/month)

**Jobs to Be Done:**

1. **"Help me understand if I can make money on TikTok"**
   - Functional: Estimate potential earnings at current follower count
   - Emotional: Feel validated that effort is worthwhile
   - Social: Justify time spent creating to friends/family

2. **"Show me what I need to hit to start earning"**
   - Functional: Calculate requirements for Creator Fund, brand deals, LIVE
   - Emotional: Feel motivated by clear milestones
   - Social: Set goals to share with creator community

3. **"Explain what all these monetization terms mean"**
   - Functional: Understand RPM, CPM, engagement rate, Creator Fund
   - Emotional: Feel less overwhelmed and confused
   - Social: Speak knowledgeably in creator spaces

**Pain Points:**
- Sees viral creators earning millions, doesn't know if it's realistic
- Confused by TikTok's multiple monetization streams
- Doesn't know which metrics matter
- Overwhelmed by conflicting advice online
- Mobile-first, impatient with slow sites

**Success Metrics:**
- Understands 3+ monetization options after visit
- Can calculate earnings potential in <60 seconds
- Returns to recalculate as follower count grows
- Shares results with creator friends

**Content Needs:**
- Simple explanations with zero jargon
- Realistic benchmarks ("creators with 10K followers typically earn...")
- Mobile-optimized calculators with big touch targets
- Motivational framing

### Persona 2: The Scaling Creator

**Demographics:**
- Age: 22–35
- Followers: 50,000–500,000
- Content: Educational, niche expertise, lifestyle
- Location: United States, UK, Canada
- Device: 60% mobile, 40% desktop

**Psychographics:**
- Treats content as business or side hustle
- Actively monetizing through multiple streams
- Compares opportunities across platforms
- Data-driven decision maker
- Income goal: Primary/supplementary income ($2K–$20K/month)

**Jobs to Be Done:**

1. **"Help me optimize my monetization mix"**
   - Functional: Compare earnings from Creator Fund vs brand deals vs LIVE vs TikTok Shop
   - Emotional: Feel confident making strategic decisions
   - Social: Position self as savvy business person

2. **"Show me if brand deal rates I'm offered are fair"**
   - Functional: Calculate market rate based on engagement, niche, follower count
   - Emotional: Feel empowered in negotiations
   - Social: Demonstrate professionalism to brands

3. **"Help me project earnings growth"**
   - Functional: Estimate earnings at different follower milestones
   - Emotional: Feel motivated to invest in growth
   - Social: Justify business investments (equipment, editing software)

4. **"Benchmark my performance against similar creators"**
   - Functional: Compare engagement rate, RPM to niche averages
   - Emotional: Validate performance or identify gaps
   - Social: Understand competitive position

**Pain Points:**
- Hard to compare opportunities across monetization streams
- Brands offer rates that feel arbitrary
- Doesn't know if focus should be growing followers or engagement
- Time-intensive to research benchmarks
- Needs data to make business decisions

**Success Metrics:**
- Uses 3+ different calculators in single session
- Returns monthly to recalculate as metrics change
- Uses benchmark data in brand negotiations
- Refers other creators to platform

**Content Needs:**
- Detailed methodology and formulas
- Niche-specific benchmarks
- Scenario comparisons (what-if calculators)
- Professional tone with data citations

### Persona 3: The Professional Creator

**Demographics:**
- Age: 25–45
- Followers: 500,000+
- Content: High-production educational, entertainment, niche authority
- Location: United States, UK
- Device: 50/50 mobile/desktop

**Psychographics:**
- Full-time creator or business owner
- Multiple revenue streams (sponsorships, products, courses, TikTok)
- Works with manager/agent
- Sophisticated understanding of creator economy
- Income goal: Primary income ($50K–$1M+/year)

**Jobs to Be Done:**

1. **"Help me justify rate sheets to brands"**
   - Functional: Calculate data-backed rates for different content types
   - Emotional: Feel confident in pricing
   - Social: Demonstrate professionalism and market knowledge

2. **"Show me opportunity cost of different monetization strategies"**
   - Functional: Compare time/earnings across TikTok Shop vs brand deals vs Creator Fund
   - Emotional: Feel strategic and efficient
   - Social: Position as sophisticated business operator

3. **"Validate my pricing against market"**
   - Functional: Benchmark rates against industry standards
   - Emotional: Avoid imposter syndrome or underpricing
   - Social: Maintain reputation as premium creator

4. **"Research for agents/managers to use in negotiations"**
   - Functional: Arm team with data to negotiate brand deals
   - Emotional: Trust in team's ability to maximize earnings
   - Social: Present as data-driven organization

**Pain Points:**
- Underpricing leaves money on table
- Overpricing damages brand relationships
- Needs authoritative sources to cite in negotiations
- Managers/agents ask for rate justifications
- Time is valuable—needs fast, accurate tools

**Success Metrics:**
- Cites platform data in rate sheets
- Shares calculators with management team
- Returns quarterly to update projections
- Links to platform from media kits

**Content Needs:**
- Authoritative sources and citations
- Advanced calculators (CPM by niche, deliverable pricing)
- Downloadable/shareable results
- API access (future consideration)

### Persona 4: The Brand/Agency Researcher

**Demographics:**
- Role: Marketing manager, media planner, social media strategist
- Company: Brand, agency, creator marketplace platform
- Location: United States, UK, Canada
- Device: 80% desktop, 20% mobile

**Psychographics:**
- Evaluating creators for campaigns
- Researching fair market rates
- Building influencer marketing budgets
- Validating creator-provided rates
- Needs to justify spend to leadership

**Jobs to Be Done:**

1. **"Help me budget influencer campaigns"**
   - Functional: Estimate costs across creator tiers
   - Emotional: Feel confident presenting to leadership
   - Social: Demonstrate market expertise

2. **"Validate if creator rates are reasonable"**
   - Functional: Compare creator quotes to market benchmarks
   - Emotional: Avoid being overcharged
   - Social: Negotiate from position of knowledge

3. **"Understand TikTok creator economics"**
   - Functional: Learn how creators earn, what motivates them
   - Emotional: Feel informed about platform dynamics
   - Social: Speak credibly about creator partnerships

4. **"Research for RFP/brief creation"**
   - Functional: Gather rate ranges and deliverable standards
   - Emotional: Feel prepared and professional
   - Social: Submit thorough, well-researched briefs

**Pain Points:**
- Creators provide rates without justification
- Hard to know if quotes are inflated
- Leadership asks for data to support budgets
- TikTok is newer than Instagram/YouTube—less established benchmarks

**Success Metrics:**
- References platform in budget presentations
- Uses calculators to validate creator quotes
- Returns for each new campaign
- Shares with team members

**Content Needs:**
- Data sources and methodology clearly cited
- Benchmark data by niche and follower tier
- Exportable/printable results
- Professional, objective tone

## User Journey Maps

### Journey 1: Aspiring Creator (First Visit)

```
Trigger: Sees friend earning from TikTok, Googles "how much can I make on TikTok"

↓

SERP: Finds pillar article "TikTok Creator Fund Calculator"

↓

Landing: Reads intro explaining Creator Fund (50% scroll)

↓

Calculator: Inputs 15K followers, 5% engagement
Results: $50–$150/month estimate shown with explanation

↓

Context: Reads "How to increase earnings" section below calculator

↓

Explore: Clicks internal link to "How TikTok LIVE Gifts Work"

↓

Outcome: Understands 3 monetization options, bookmarks site, returns in 2 weeks
```

**Key Touchpoints to Optimize:**
- Mobile-first calculator design
- Clear, jargon-free explanations
- Contextual next steps after results
- Sticky header with other calculators
- Fast load times (attention span <3 seconds)

### Journey 2: Scaling Creator (Research Session)

```
Trigger: Brand offers $500 for sponsored video, wants to validate rate

↓

SERP: Googles "TikTok brand deal calculator"

↓

Landing: Finds "TikTok Brand Deal Rate Calculator"

↓

Calculator: Inputs 150K followers, 8% engagement, lifestyle niche
Results: $800–$1,500 recommended rate with methodology

↓

Validation: Reads "How We Calculate Rates" section

↓

Comparison: Opens second calculator in new tab (Creator Fund calculator)

↓

Decision: Uses data to counter-offer brand at $1,000

↓

Outcome: Returns monthly, shares with creator mastermind group
```

**Key Touchpoints to Optimize:**
- Desktop-optimized layouts with visible methodology
- Niche-specific benchmarks
- Multiple calculators easy to access simultaneously
- Shareable/bookmarkable result URLs (future feature)
- Professional, credible design

### Journey 3: Brand Researcher (Budget Planning)

```
Trigger: Boss assigns TikTok influencer campaign, needs budget

↓

SERP: Googles "TikTok influencer rates 2025"

↓

Landing: Finds pillar guide "Ultimate Guide to TikTok Creator Rates"

↓

Reading: Skims 5,000-word guide (table of contents navigation)

↓

Calculator: Uses "Brand Deal Calculator" for 3 scenarios:
- Micro (50K followers)
- Mid-tier (250K followers)
- Macro (1M followers)

↓

Documentation: Screenshots results for budget deck

↓

Outcome: Presents to leadership, cites source, returns for next campaign
```

**Key Touchpoints to Optimize:**
- Authoritative brand positioning
- Clearly cited sources
- Easy-to-screenshot/export results
- Professional design suitable for presentations
- Detailed methodology for credibility

## Voice & Tone by Persona

### Aspiring Creator
- **Voice:** Friendly, encouraging, educational
- **Tone:** Conversational, simple, motivational
- **Example:** "Great question! Here's the deal: with 10K followers and solid engagement, you could earn $50–$150/month from the Creator Fund. Plus, that's just one stream—LIVE gifts and brand deals can add a lot more."

### Scaling Creator
- **Voice:** Professional, data-driven, strategic
- **Tone:** Confident, detailed, actionable
- **Example:** "Based on your 150K followers and 8% engagement rate in the lifestyle niche, market rates for a single in-feed video range from $800–$1,500. Here's how we calculate that..."

### Professional Creator
- **Voice:** Authoritative, sophisticated, efficient
- **Tone:** Concise, credible, citation-backed
- **Example:** "According to Q4 2024 industry data, creators in your tier (500K+, lifestyle niche) command $3,000–$6,000 per deliverable. Our methodology draws from 1,000+ verified brand deals."

### Brand Researcher
- **Voice:** Objective, professional, transparent
- **Tone:** Analytical, unbiased, well-sourced
- **Example:** "This calculator uses verified market data from 2,500+ TikTok influencer campaigns to estimate fair market rates. All formulas and sources are documented below."
